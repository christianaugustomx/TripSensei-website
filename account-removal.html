Account Removal

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Summary: You can request complete removal of your TripSensei account and all associated data at any time. Requests are processed within 48 hours.

How to request account removal

You can request removal of your account using any of these methods:

  • Via WhatsApp: Send the message DELETE MY DATA to our WhatsApp agent at +1 (415) 423-0178.
  • Via email: Send an email to hola@tripsensei.com with the subject line "Account Removal Request". Include the phone number associated with your account.

What data is deleted

When your account is removed, we permanently delete:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number and profile association.
  • Trip information — destinations, dates, and preferences.
  • Conversation history — all messages exchanged with the agent.
  • Tip ratings and feedback you provided.
  • Subscription status and plan information.

What is NOT deleted

The following may be retained for legal or operational reasons:

  • Payment transaction records — retained by our payment processor (Stripe) as required by financial regulations. TripSensei does not store your card details.
  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics — statistical data that cannot be linked back to you.

Please note: Account removal is permanent and cannot be undone. After deletion, you will no longer receive travel tips or alerts. You can always start a new conversation with TripSensei at any time.

Processing time

Account removal requests are processed within 48 hours. You will receive a confirmation message on WhatsApp (or email) once your data has been deleted.

Questions?

If you have questions about your data or this process, review our Privacy Policy or contact us:

Contact us

Email hola@tripsensei.com or message us on WhatsApp. We respond within 24 hours.
